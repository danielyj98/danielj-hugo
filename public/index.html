Experience

At Mesh Systems, I worked as a Software Engineer from June 2023 to July 2024, primarily focusing on developing and maintaining user interfaces (UIs) and backend-for-frontend (BFF) services in an Agile environment. I led the creation of a web portal for Panasonic Sonic Steamer microwaves, enabling users to manage and edit preset recipes with a user-friendly interface and scalable infrastructure. Additionally, I developed an Alarm Dashboard UI for IoT device operators to monitor and manage device fleets, providing tools for predictive maintenance and intuitive visualization. I also contributed to an internal tool for automating repository deployments, streamlining the setup and customization process for delivery teams. Throughout my tenure, I utilized technologies such as Node.js, JavaScript, React, Redux, Microsoft Azure, and more. I was recognized for my eagerness, product quality, and flexibility, and successfully overcame challenges as the sole front-end developer for multiple projects.
